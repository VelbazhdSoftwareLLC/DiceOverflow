Initial release of Dice Overflow - a strategic board logical dice game.

Features:
• Strategic dice-based gameplay
• AI opponent with intelligent moves
• Clean and intuitive user interface
• Settings for customization
• Help section with game rules
• Sound effects and visual feedback

This game was developed as a master thesis project at New Bulgarian University, exploring artificial intelligence and game theory concepts in a practical gaming application.
