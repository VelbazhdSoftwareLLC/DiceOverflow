Dice Overflow is a simple board logical game developed as a master thesis in New Bulgarian University.

This strategic dice game challenges your logical thinking and planning skills. Place dice on the board and try to outsmart your opponent by creating the best combinations. The game features:

• Strategic gameplay that requires careful planning
• Clean and intuitive interface
• AI opponent with different difficulty levels
• Settings to customize your gaming experience
• Help section to learn the rules and strategies

Whether you're a casual player looking for a quick game or someone who enjoys strategic thinking, Dice Overflow offers an engaging experience that will keep you coming back for more.

The game was originally developed as an academic project to explore artificial intelligence and game theory concepts, making it both entertaining and educational.
